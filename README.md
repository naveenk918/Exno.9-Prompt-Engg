##  Aim
To explore and understand various prompting techniques used for generating and refining videos through AI models.

---

##  Algorithm / Procedure

1. Explore AI tools capable of video generation from text prompts such as:
   - Runway Gen-2

2. Begin with a base prompt and analyze the generated video output.  
3. Refine the same base prompt with more descriptive details, cinematic elements, and artistic styling.  
4. Observe and compare how the output changes with each refinement.  
5. Document the results and conclusions.

---

##  Base Prompt

> **"A person in a red jacket walking along a sunny park path, with birds flying in the sky and a dog running beside them."**

---

##  Prompt Refinement Types

###  1. Detailed Prompt
> "A person in a bright red jacket walking along a sunny park pathway lined with green trees, while birds soar gently in the blue sky and a golden retriever joyfully runs beside them."

**Expected Output:**  
A realistic, cheerful video with detailed background elements and natural outdoor lighting.

---

###  2. Cinematic Prompt
> "A cinematic shot of a person in a red jacket walking along a sunlit park path, captured with a slow panning camera. Birds fly across the frame as a golden retriever runs beside, with soft background music and warm evening lighting."

**Expected Output:**  
A movie-style sequence with camera motion, enhanced lighting, and emotional tone.

---

###  3. Artistic Prompt
> "An animated watercolor-style video of a person wearing a red jacket walking along a bright park path, with softly painted birds flying above and a sketch-like dog joyfully following them under a gentle sun."

**Expected Output:**  
A stylized, animated clip with artistic brushstroke textures and smooth motion.

---
